"Runners for Victory"

In the army I established a project called "Runners for Victory". The goal of the project was to return wounded soldiers to fitness. First I collected all the names of the wounded soldiers. I sat with each and every soldier and tried to understand where he was hurting and the intensity of the pain. Based on the information I gathered, I built a personalized training plan for each soldier. And I was following them. The program resulted in impressive achievements and returned soldiers to running, training, carrying weights.
